## Scope (“What this Privacy Policy covers”)

The Policy covers how Replay treats **Personal Data** gathered when you access or use the Services. **Personal Data** means information that identifies or relates to a particular individual, including categories called “personally identifiable information” or “personal information” under applicable laws. It does **not** cover companies Replay doesn’t own or control, or people Replay doesn’t manage.

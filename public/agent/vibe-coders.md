## Debugging tools were built for engineers. You’re not one. That’s fine.

Most debugging tools assume you know what a network request or console log means. If you’re building with **Lovable, Base44, Replit**, or similar, the page argues you shouldn’t need to.

**Replay doesn’t ask you to understand the technical details. It tells you what broke and how to fix it.**

---

## Three steps. No setup.

1. **Install the Replay Chrome Extension** — About 30 seconds from the Chrome Web Store.  
2. **Go to your app, hit Record, and reproduce the bug** — Do the broken flow while Replay is watching (clicks, forms, etc.).  
3. **Read what Replay found** — Plain-English explanation and fix; **copy the fix and paste it into your AI tool.**

Supporting line: You don’t need complicated installs or new tools.
